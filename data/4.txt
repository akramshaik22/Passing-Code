Input format:
First line: n number of characters in each string.
Second line: Two strings in new line.

Output format:
YES, if strings are anagrams else NO.

Constrains:
1 <= n <= 10^3
Time: 1 sec

  Input1:
  4
  race
  care

  output1:
  YES

  Input2:
  5
  hello
  world

  output2:
  NO
